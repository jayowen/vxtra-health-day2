# Vxtra Health: PEACE Sound Bites

*Finalized Feb 11, 2026 | Day 2 Workshop*

---

## PEACE Framework

| Letter | Purpose | What It Does |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **P** | Problem | Gets their attention (7 words or fewer) |
| **E** | Empathy | Show you understand. Create a bond. |
| **A** | Answer | Position Vxtra as the solution. |
| **C** | Change | Aspirational identity. Who they become. |
| **E** | End Result | Happy ever after vision. Brand promise. |

---

## 1. THE EMPLOYER (Self-Funded C-Suite / HR Leader)

**P: Problem:**
"Health insurance costs are crushing your business."

**E: Empathy:**
"You shouldn't have to choose between taking care of your people and protecting your bottom line."

**A: Answer:**
"Vxtra Health gives your company access to the same healthcare technology and drug pricing that only Fortune 100 companies have had, with transparent data, local physicians, and costs you can actually track and control."

**C: Change:**
"Be the reason your company finally has health insurance it can be proud of."

**E: End Result:**
"Your employees get better care from physicians they trust, your costs become predictable and your data becomes transparent."

---

## 2. THE BROKER (Insurance Broker / Benefits Advisor)

**P: Problem:**
"It's hard to differentiate yourself in the marketplace. Everyone is selling the same thing."

**E: Empathy:**
"It's exhausting trying to stand out when you're selling the same solutions as every other broker in town."

**A: Answer:**
"Vxtra Health gives you a transparent health plan backed by Fortune 100 technology that your competitors don't have access to."

**C: Change:**
"Be the broker who brings something nobody else can offer."

**E: End Result:**
"Your clients save real money, your book of business is protected, and employers come to you because you're the one with the plan that actually works."

---

## 3. THE PHYSICIAN

**P: Problem:**
"Insurance companies are practicing medicine for you."

**E: Empathy:**
"You didn't spend a decade in training to fight pre-authorization forms and get told how to treat your own patients by someone who's never seen them."

**A: Answer:**
"Vxtra Health puts physicians, not insurance companies, back in charge of patient care. You work directly with local employers and their employees, supported by technology that handles the claims and data so you can focus on medicine."

**C: Change:**
"Practice medicine the way you always intended to."

**E: End Result:**
"Your patients trust you, not a call center. You make the clinical decisions. And you run an independent practice in a community that knows your name."

---

## 4. THE EMPLOYEE (End Beneficiary)

**P: Problem:**
"Healthcare is confusing and way too expensive."

**E: Empathy:**
"Nobody should dread going to the doctor because they're afraid of what it'll cost."

**A: Answer:**
"Your employer has partnered with Vxtra Health to connect you with local physicians who actually know you, with lower out-of-pocket costs and none of the insurance runaround."

**C: Change:**
"Be someone who actually uses their healthcare, without the stress."

**E: End Result:**
"You call your doctor, you get an appointment, you get the care you need, and you don't dread opening the bill."
