Critical Constraint

Phase 1 vs. Phase 2 Messaging

Vxtra must operate within the existing Cigna network in Augusta for the initial launch. This shapes everything we say publicly until proprietary networks are live.

Phase 1: Now – Mid-2027

Pro-employer. Pro-transparency. Solution-forward.

  • “We built a better way for employers and doctors”
  • “Fortune 100 technology, now available to you”
  • “Transparent data, local physicians, predictable costs”
  • Reference Capital Rx capabilities and partnerships
  • Share cost trends and data delays (factual, not accusatory)

Do NOT name Cigna, BCBS, United, or Aetna negatively. Do not use “hijacked” or “broken” in public materials.

Phase 2: Mid-2027 Onward

Direct contrast. Data-backed. Authoritative.

  • Real dollar savings from Augusta data
  • Direct carrier comparisons using Vxtra’s own performance data
  • Employee and physician testimonials
  • Larry’s full thought leadership voice unlocked
  • Case studies with named employers

Proprietary networks live. Cigna dependency removed. Stronger positioning becomes viable.

Partnership Recommendation

Why fractional CMO is the right model for Vxtra’s first 12 months

The Case for Fractional

Why This Model Over a Full-Time Hire

Cost Efficiency

A full-time VP of Marketing or CMO costs $150K–$250K+ in salary and benefits. A fractional engagement delivers senior strategic leadership at a fraction of that cost — critical when working capital is $2M and every dollar needs to drive toward 500 enrolled employees.

Immediate Capability

No 3-month ramp-up period. Business Builders already has full context from the StoryBrand workshop, understands the messaging, knows the personas, and has the production team ready to execute. Day one is productive.

Full Team, Not One Person

A fractional model through Business Builders provides a strategist, designer, developer, copywriter, and video coordinator — not a single marketing generalist trying to do everything alone.

Flexibility

As Vxtra grows and funding scales (private placement $3.5M in Q3, additional markets in 2027), the engagement can scale up or transition to an in-house hire supported by the agency. The fractional model bridges the gap.

Sandra’s Bandwidth

Sandra is running full-time operations and no one on the Vxtra team is dedicated to marketing. A fractional partner handles execution so the Vxtra team can focus on building the health plan and serving employers.

Ready to bring the blueprint to life?

We’ll discuss scope, timeline, and investment during the follow-up call. The right time to evaluate a full-time marketing hire is after Augusta has proven the model (Q1 2027) and Gainesville launch planning demands dedicated in-house coordination.
