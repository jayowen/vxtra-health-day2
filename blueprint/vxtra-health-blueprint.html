Executive Summary

Over two days in St. Augustine, FL, the Vxtra Health leadership team and Business Builders worked through a comprehensive StoryBrand Blueprint workshop, identifying audiences, defining the villain, building messaging frameworks, and creating the strategic foundation for all future marketing.

The StoryBrand 7-Part Framework

Character → Problem → Guide → Plan → CTA → Failure / Success

The Villain

Health insurance companies

“Health insurance companies have hijacked healthcare and make it very difficult for doctors and nurses to do what they do best.”

- Larry Hightower

Four Audiences Identified

Hero #1

Employers

CEO or HR leader, 200–2,000 employees. Want transparent costs and a better experience for everyone.

Hero #2

Brokers

Independent benefits advisors. Want a product the competition can't match.

Enabler

Physicians

Want their agency restored. Buried in pre-authorizations and insurance bureaucracy.

Beneficiary

Employees

The end beneficiary. Want care without fear, confusion, or financial stress.

Key Strategic Decisions

Positioning

Vxtra owns the problem of health insurance costs + terrible experience, not "healthcare is broken."

Go-To-Market

Employers are the primary hero. Brokers are the gateway. Physicians come second as the model proves itself.

Primary CTA

“Schedule a Call” for employers and brokers. “Join the Network” for physicians.

Core Differentiator

The “Our Town” model: local employers + local physicians + Fortune 100 technology, in secondary markets.

Messaging Rules

Always Say

“Physician” or “Doctor”: never “provider”

“Educate without offending”

Dollar amounts, not percentages

“Our money” (not “my money”)

“Avoidable cost” (not fraud/waste)

Never Say

“Provider”: “They’re not providers. They’re physicians.”

“Healthcare is broken”: the problem is how it’s paid for

Insurance jargon (EOB, TPA, PBM, NADAC)

High cognitive load sentences

Employer Messaging Framework

Character

A CEO or HR leader in a company with 200 to 2,000 employees.

Want: Transparent costs and a better experience for everyone.

Problem

External: Health insurance costs too much and the experience is terrible for everyone

Internal: Trapped, frustrated, and powerless

Philosophical: “It’s just plain wrong that it’s so expensive and the experience is so bad for everyone.”

Guide

Empathy: “We know how frustrating the entire insurance industry can be.”

Authority: Decades of experience on both sides of the table. 30+ years delivering healthcare and financing it. Fortune 100 technology, transparent data.

Plan

1. Schedule a conversation

2. See what you qualify for

3. Be free from the chains of traditional insurance options

CTA: “Schedule a Call”

Failure

• Costs compound year over year in all the wrong ways

• Top talent leaves (68% cite benefits)

• Employees miss more work due to inefficient health insurance

• No one is happy with the experience of using their health insurance when they really need it

Success

• Predictable, lower costs

• Transparent data

• Hire and retain top talent

Transformation: From trapped → Free. From dreading renewal season → Confident in predictable costs. From powerless → Leading the change.

One-Liner

Health insurance costs are increasing at an unsustainable rate, leaving employers feeling trapped. Vxtra Health provides a way out with a tech-forward platform that connects with the best local physicians to achieve predictable costs and better outcomes.

Schedule a call today, and find out if Vxtra is the right fit for your company.
